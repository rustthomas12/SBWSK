    Time until the first content appears on screen

    Good: 0-1.8s | Needs Improvement: 1.8-3s | Poor: 3s+

    Largest Contentful Paint (LCP)

    Time until the largest content element is visible

    Good: 0-2.5s | Needs Improvement: 2.5-4s | Poor: 4s+

    Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS)

    Measures visual stability (unexpected layout shifts)

    Good: 0-0.1 | Needs Improvement: 0.1-0.25 | Poor: 0.25+

    Time to Interactive (TTI)

    Time until the page is fully interactive

    Good: 0-3.8s | Needs Improvement: 3.8-7.3s | Poor: 7.3s+

    Total Blocking Time (TBT)

    Time the main thread is blocked from responding to input

    Good: 0-200ms | Needs Improvement: 200-600ms | Poor: 600ms+
