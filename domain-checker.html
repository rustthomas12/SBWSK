Why Domain Names Matter

  • Your domain is your online identity and brand
  • A memorable domain makes it easy for customers to find you
  • .com domains are still the most trusted and recognized

ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Choosing a Domain

  • Keep it short and memorable (ideally under 15 characters)
  • Try adding your location (e.g., "mybusinessboston")
  • Use your industry keywords (e.g., "mybusinessplumbing")
  • Add action words (e.g., "getmybusiness", "mybusinessnow")
  • Consider abbreviations or creative spellings
  • Avoid numbers and hyphens when possible
