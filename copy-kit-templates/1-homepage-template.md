## üí° QUICK TIPS FOR GREAT HOMEPAGE COPY

‚úÖ Focus on **benefits** (what they get) not features (what you do)
‚úÖ Use "you" and "your" more than "we" and "our"
‚úÖ Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)
‚úÖ Use bullet points for easy scanning
‚úÖ Include at least 2 calls-to-action
‚úÖ Add real client testimonials when possible
‚úÖ Use specific numbers ("Save 10 hours/week" vs "Save time")
‚úÖ Answer: "Why should I choose you over competitors?"
