=====================================================
CONTACT PAGE BEST PRACTICES
=====================================================

✓ Keep the form short (3-5 fields max)
✓ Don't hide your contact info - make it obvious
✓ Set clear expectations for response time
✓ Offer multiple contact methods
✓ Make it mobile-friendly
✓ Add a compelling reason to contact you
✓ Include a photo of you/your team (builds trust)
✓ Test your form regularly
✓ Send an auto-reply confirming receipt
✓ Respond quickly (within 24 hours)
