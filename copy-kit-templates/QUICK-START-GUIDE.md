## ğŸ’¡ TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Do's âœ…
- **Be specific** - Use numbers, timelines, and concrete details
- **Focus on benefits** - What customers GET, not what you DO
- **Use "you/your"** - Speak directly to visitors
- **Keep it short** - Short sentences and paragraphs
- **Add personality** - Let your unique voice shine
- **Include proof** - Testimonials, numbers, results
- **Have clear CTAs** - Tell people what to do next
- **Edit ruthlessly** - Remove unnecessary words

### Don'ts âŒ
- Don't use jargon or industry buzzwords
- Don't make it all about you ("we, we, we")
- Don't write long paragraphs
- Don't forget to proofread
- Don't skip the testimonials section
- Don't be vague ("quality service")
- Don't forget mobile readers
