=====================================================
ABOUT PAGE BEST PRACTICES
=====================================================

✓ Use photos! People buy from people
✓ Be authentic - show your personality
✓ Focus on how you help THEM, not just your credentials
✓ Tell stories, not just facts
✓ Keep it scannable with headers and short paragraphs
✓ Include a clear next step (CTA)
✓ Update regularly with recent achievements
✓ Show, don't just tell ("Worked with 50+ clients" vs "Experienced")
