=====================================================
QUICK TIPS FOR GREAT HOMEPAGE COPY
=====================================================

✓ Focus on benefits (what they get) not features (what you do)
✓ Use "you" and "your" more than "we" and "our"
✓ Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)
✓ Use bullet points for easy scanning
✓ Include at least 2 calls-to-action
✓ Add real client testimonials when possible
✓ Use specific numbers ("Save 10 hours/week" vs "Save time")
✓ Answer: "Why should I choose you over competitors?"
